### Project Description

Basically, this app is designed to reward you for wasting time on your smartphone...
- It is the opposite to digital wellbeing

### The Problem (that doesn't exist)

People keep trying to reduce their smartphone usage, but some of us want to embrace the chaos! There‚Äôs no app out there that rewards you for spending more time on your phone, browsing memes, watching cat videos, or scrolling endlessly.

### The Solution (that nobody asked for)

- Screen King rewards you for your epic dedication to phone usage. The more time you spend, the more points you earn!
